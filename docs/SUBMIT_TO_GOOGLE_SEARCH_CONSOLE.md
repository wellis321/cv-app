# How to Submit Your Sitemap to Google Search Console

## Step 1: Access Google Search Console
1. Go to https://search.google.com/search-console
2. Log in with the same Google account you used for the verification code

## Step 2: Select Your Property
1. You should see "simple-cv-builder.com" in your list of properties
2. Click on it to open the dashboard

## Step 3: Submit the Sitemap
1. In the left sidebar, click on **"Sitemaps"**
2. You'll see a section called "Add a new sitemap"
3. In the input field, type exactly: **`sitemap.xml`**
4. Click the **"Submit"** button

## What Happens Next
- Google will start crawling your sitemap
- You'll see "Success" when it's submitted
- Within a few days, Google will start indexing your pages
- You can check the status by returning to the Sitemaps section

## Your Sitemap URL
Your sitemap is now available at: **https://simple-cv-builder.com/sitemap.xml**

You can visit this URL to see your sitemap in action!

## Additional Benefits
✅ Created robots.txt at /robots.txt
