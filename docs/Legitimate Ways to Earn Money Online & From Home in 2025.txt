            <div class="earning-potential">Earning Potential: £50-£300+ per month</div>

                <li><strong>Toluna</strong> - Mix of surveys and product testing opportunities</li>

        <h2>Important Considerations</h2>

        <div class="warning-box">

            <p>Any income earned—whether online or offline—may need to be declared to HMRC. As a general rule:</p>

                <li>Consider consulting an accountant if earnings become substantial</li>

        </div>

            <p><strong>Invest in skills:</strong> Higher-paying opportunities usually require more specialized skills. Consider investing time in learning valuable skills like writing, design, or coding.</p>

            <p><strong>Track everything:</strong> Keep a spreadsheet of time invested versus earnings for each method. Focus on what delivers the best return on your time.</p>

            <p><strong>Be patient:</strong> Building substantial online income takes time. Most people start earning £50-£200 per month and scale up over 6-12 months.</p>
